About

Nintendo was very much a large part of my childhood, I loved to play games with my brothers and sisters, my first favourite was Super Mario World and then later when the Game Boy was released, Pokemon became an instant favourite, so expect to see plenty of pokemon titles pop up on these pages!

You may notice that not all the consoles are shown in the navigation bar, these will be updated soon!

On each page you will find information about each console, my favourite games on that particular console and my personal rankings, if you have suggestions on any games that I did not mention, that you think I should play some time, or you have any queries about games that I have mentioned, feel free to contact me!
